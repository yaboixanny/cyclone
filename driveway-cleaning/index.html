Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about our residential driveway cleaning services in Sacramento.

Why should I invest in professional residential driveway cleaning?

Your driveway is one of the first things neighbors and guests see when they visit your home. Professional driveway cleaning eliminates unsightly oil stains, embedded tire marks, algae, and built-up grime — instantly boosting your home's curb appeal and protecting the surface from long-term deterioration.

How often should I schedule residential driveway cleaning?

For most residential driveways in Sacramento, an annual or bi-annual deep clean keeps things looking great and prevents stains from deeply penetrating the surface. Homes with multiple vehicles, heavy oil drips, or shaded driveways prone to algae and moss may benefit from more frequent seasonal cleanings.

Can you fully remove old oil stains from my home driveway?

Yes! We use professional-grade, biodegradable degreasers specifically formulated to break down motor oil, hydraulic fluid, and fuel stains before our high-pressure equipment blasts them away. While extremely old or deeply set stains may lighten rather than vanish completely, the improvement is always significant and consistently impressive to homeowners.

Will driveway cleaning damage the asphalt or concrete surface?

Not at all. Our technicians are trained to calibrate pressure levels precisely based on your driveway's surface — whether it's asphalt, stamped concrete, pavers, or standard concrete. We use surface cleaning attachments that deliver even, controlled pressure across every square foot without gouging or etching your surface.

Do I need to do anything to prepare my driveway before the cleaning crew arrives?

Just make sure the driveway is clear of parked vehicles and any loose items before we arrive — that's really all we need on your end! Our crew handles the full pre-treatment, washing, and post-cleanup so your driveway looks immaculate by the time we pack up and leave.
